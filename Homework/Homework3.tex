    $$Y_{22}'=Y_{22}+(.01+.15j)^{-1}+.11j$$

    $$Y_{22}'=2.6025-35.6312j[p.u.]$$
    $$Y_{44}'=2.1345-29.4042j[p.u.]$$

    $$Z_{eq}=\frac{(.01+.15j)^{2}}{2(.01+.15j)}$$
    $$Y_{eq}=\frac{2(.01+.15j)}{(.01+.15j)^{2}}$$
    $$Y_{eq}=\frac{2}{(.01+.15j)}$$
